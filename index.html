Baseline
What is Baseline?
A free business health tool for entrepreneurs and small business owners. No sign-up. No data stored anywhere but your device. Built to help you understand your numbers — without a finance degree.
1

Set up in 3 steps

Tell us your name, your business, and what you sell. That's it. You're in under 2 minutes — no email, no password, nothing to install.

2

Download your free Baseline Logbook

Get a spreadsheet pre-filled with your product names. Record every sale, expense, and stock movement daily — one row per entry. Keep it in your phone or Google Drive.

3

Log your transactions (your way)

Use the Logbook in Excel or Google Sheets, or log directly inside Baseline using Fill My Log. Either approach works — your data stays on your device.

4

Fill your Ledger — or let Baseline do it

The Ledger is your monthly business snapshot. Upload your filled Logbook and it auto-populates. Or enter your numbers directly. Once your Ledger is filled, Baseline has everything it needs.

5

Calculate your Baseline metrics

Run 17 health metrics — contribution margin, break-even, gross margin, working capital, inventory turnover, and more. Each result comes with a plain-language explanation of what it means for your business.
